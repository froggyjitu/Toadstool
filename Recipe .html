Chocholate Chips Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Butter or margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar, packed
  • 2 Eggs, large
  • 2 teaspoon Vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups of Flour, unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon Baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 cup Walnuts or pecans, chopped (optional)
  • 1 cup KCC Bittersweet 60% Cocoa Baking Chips

Baking Instruction

  1. Heat oven at 375'F
  2. Stir flour with baking soda and salt; set aside.
  3. In large mixing bowl, beat butter with sugar, and brown sugar at medium speed until creamy and lightened in color.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla, one at a time.
    Mix on low speed untill incorporated.
  5. Gradually blend dry mixure into creamed mixture.
    Stir in nuts and chocolate chips.
    6. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  6. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until chocolate chip cookies are golden brown.
  7. Enjoy.
